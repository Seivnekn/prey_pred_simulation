Prey/Predator Environment Simulation

This simulation represents a simple predator/prey relationship.
The smaller entities pursue only food.
The larger entities pursue only the smaller entities.

Food entities appear as small static blocks over the map.
A new food spawns each second, and they despawn when eaten.

All entities have base roaming velocities and an elevated velocity, either chasing for predators or escaping for prey.

In this phase, the prey entities can be seen as green when in a normal state; They become red when escaping.
The predator entity appears magenta when normal, and yellow when chasing.
